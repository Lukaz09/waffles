Family Baking Origins

Personal Story:

It began when I first drank mountain dew. I thought that it was the greatest thing I had ever tasted. The flavors exploded across every tastebud and the fizziness bubbled across every inch of my tongue. But the I couldn't breathe. I had a strong allergic reaction to mountain dew. I was rushed to the hospital and told I could never have it unless it was stored/soaked in some other carbohydrate. I tried soaking it in bread, but it wasn't nearly the same feeling or taste. I then started up baking, to put my efforts towards substitutes that I could make my mountain dew into. In my search, there were no mountain dew baking recipes, because of course there wouldn't be, why would there. So, I went and searched. I dove to the depths of the pacific and extracted ice from magical glaciers. And the most difficult to get, was the extracted dew from the Lime Spiders.



In all my travels for the most magical ingredients in the entire lands, I found the Lime Spider. Containing the dew needed for mountain dew a quality 1,000 times higher than that of normal and average mountain dew. It's smell alone made me see heaven. I began to work. I found countless other ingredients like from the gallery page. These allowed me to craft heavenly pieces of art. Deserts. True deserts. The plethora of deserts I have made thus far is not far nor wide, but each one enhances life as a whole and makes you feel as if you have changed entirely.
